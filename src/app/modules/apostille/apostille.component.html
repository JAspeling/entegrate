Guidelines for doing it yourself
Department of Home Affairs

Go to the nearest South African Department of Home Affairs office or the office responsible for issuing these documents. They will have the necessary forms and information on the process.

Application form

Fill out the application form for an apostille on the specific document(s) you require. Provide accurate information and ensure that all fields are correctly filled.

Pay the fee

There may be a fee associated with obtaining the apostille. The exact amount will vary, so check with the relevant authority for the current fee schedule. Make the payment as required.

Submit the application

Submit the completed application form, along with the original birth certificate and/or marriage certificate, and any other requested documents, to the Department of Home Affairs.

Wait for processing

The processing time for apostilles can vary, so it's advisable to inquire about the estimated processing time when submitting the application. It could take several weeks to receive the apostilled documents.

Collect the documents

Once the apostilles are issued, you can collect the documents from the Department of Home Affairs. Ensure that you receive the original documents with the apostille attached.
