In preparation for your move, there are some documents that you need to get in order.

Timeline

This usually takes only a few days. You can get it all done in one day.

Get these documents ready
  • Passport
  • Higher education certificate
  • Passport photos
Passport
You are leaving South Africa, and for that, you need a valid passport. This is something that can easily slip your mind!

Make sure your passport is valid for more than 6 months upon arrival. If your passport is valid for less than a year, I suggest you get it renewed.

*Please make sure that the passports are up to date for everyone coming along.
Education certificate
This will have to be submitted to Entelect. Just make sure you have copies of your degree, or Higher education certificate(s) so that they are ready upon request.
Passport photos

You will need these when you go to the embassy There is a specific format that Netherlands require for these photos. Kodak is aware of these requirements, so head over there and make sure to ask them for the format that the Netherlands require

You will also be able to use these in the Netherlands when applying for your drivers license, so you can get 6 - 10 passport photos per person
